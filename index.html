About

I am Hung-Ting Chen, a student just graduated and received B.S.E from National Taiwan University and intended to apply for graduate programs in the United States for Fall 2021. Currently I work as research assistant in Chinese Knowledge and Information Processing (CKIP) Laboratory. Under the supervision of Dr. Wei-Yun Ma, I work on natural language processing(NLP) tasks, mainly focusing on language generation.

Before that, I worked with Prof. Lin-Shan Lee and Hung-Yi Lee on automatic text summarization and cross-accent speech recognition. You can find detailed descriptions of the works below.

I am also interested in solving NLP tasks in data-scarce scenarios and apply NLP techniques on web information, such as machine translation of webpage contents or summarization of social media posts, just to name a few.
