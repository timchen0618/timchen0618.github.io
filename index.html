About

I am Hung-Ting Chen, a student just graduated and received B.S.E from National Taiwan University. I intend to apply for graduate programs in the United States for Fall 2021. Currently I work as research assistant in Chinese Knowledge and Information Processing (CKIP) Laboratory under the supervision of Dr. Wei-Yun Ma. My projects include data-to-text generation (ongoing) and pattern-guided dialogue generation (published to DEEP-DAIL2021 workshop at AAAI 2021).

Before that, I worked with Prof. Lin-Shan Lee and Hung-Yi Lee on abstractive text summarization and meta-learning for automatic speech recognition. You can find links to the projects below.
